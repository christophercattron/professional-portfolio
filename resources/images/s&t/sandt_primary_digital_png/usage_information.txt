The names, logos and symbols available on brand.mst.edu are owned by Missouri University of Science and Technology. After reviewing the guidelines, faculty, staff and students can use these logos for official university business purposes only. They may also be used for non-commercial purposes at university-sponsored events.

Non-university third parties that would like to use the university's name, symbols, or logos, or who would like to demonstrate an affiliation or partnership with Missouri S&T, must request permission to use the logos by emailing marketing@mst.edu or by calling the Missouri S&T Marketing and Communications office at 573-341-4328.
